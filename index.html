About

I am a research associate in semantics at the University of Potsdam (PhD position). My main research project is on demonstratives in Spanish and Italian — in particular, how "defective" demonstratives in these languages can inform a more general theory of indexical expressions.

Other interests include definiteness in general (in languages such as Akan), and embedded interrogatives, which I investigated experimentally as a member of the project Exhaustiveness in embedded questions (ExQ).
